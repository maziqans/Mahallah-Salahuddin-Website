Important things to know in Mahallah

Check in & Check out Room

Compulsory to check in at the beginning of the semester and check out at the end of the semester.

Vacate & clean the room and surrender the room key at the end of every semester.

Live In During Vacation (LIDV)

Application for group/official LIDV is to be submitted to RSD/mahallah office within the stated date.

LIDV for personal reason and late request for LIDV are considered as rental.

Students who fail to apply LIDV and vacate the room and surrender the room key at the end of the semester will be given a compound and will be charged for LIDV.

Vacation Notice (Available in I-Maalum, Fb & RSD Website)

Mahallah Online Room Registration (MORR)

All students who will register for the coming semester are required to do MORR at the stated date in the vacation notice (2 weeks before the final exam).

Failure to do MORR may cause students to have no room for the next semester, and students have to vacate their room and surrender the room key.

Students who are not eligible to do MORR due to: no registered subject, outstanding fees, double degree, exceed 4 years, practical training & FYP need to fill in details in the google form (bit.ly/morr_sem1 or scan QR code).
