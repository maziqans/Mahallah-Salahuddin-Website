Cafe

Shawarma Habibi

Welcome to Shawarma Habibi, where the rich flavors of the Middle East come to life in every bite. Immerse yourself in the vibrant and inviting atmosphere as you savor our authentic shawarma, meticulously crafted with a blend of premium meats, aromatic spices, and traditional recipes passed down through generations. Our menu boasts a tantalizing selection of shawarma wraps, bowls, and platters, each offering a delightful journey for your taste buds. At Shawarma Habibi, we take pride in delivering a culinary experience that captures the essence of hospitality and love – a true celebration of Middle Eastern flavors. Join us and indulge in the taste of tradition, where every dish tells a story of heritage and passion.

Service Hours 7:30 AM - 11:00 PM

Court

Multipurpose Court

Experience the thrill of sports and recreation at our Multipurpose Court facility – the perfect destination for enthusiasts seeking versatility in their active pursuits. Our state-of-the-art court is a dynamic space designed to accommodate various sports and recreational activities. Whether you're into basketball, volleyball, badminton, or any other court-based sport, our multipurpose court provides the ideal setting for friendly competitions and engaging workouts. Equipped with top-notch amenities, our court ensures a seamless blend of fitness and fun. Embrace an active lifestyle and join us at the Multipurpose Court, where versatility meets excellence, and every game is an opportunity for a new adventure.

Service Hours 6:00 AM - 12:00 AM

Surau

Musolla

Experience the tranquility of our Musolla, a sacred space for Salah and spiritual connection. Our Musolla, or prayer room, is designed as a serene sanctuary for reflection, contemplation, and devotion. Embrace the peaceful ambiance as you engage in moments of connection with the Divine. Tailored for various prayer needs, the Musolla is a haven for individuals seeking solace and a tranquil refuge for Salah. Find your inner peace and deepen your spiritual journey at our Musolla – a space dedicated to the practice of Islamic rituals and mindful contemplation.

Service Hours Opens 24 Hours
